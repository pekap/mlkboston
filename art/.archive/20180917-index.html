We have selected five exceptional artists and teams who will develop memorial proposals during the summer of 2018. Their renderings will be displayed at The Boston Public Library for the month of September, and the winner will be announced in November 2018, and then will be commissioned to build the memorial. Here are the five finalists selected from over 100 applications:

  • David Adjaye and Adam Pendleton with Future\Pace
  • Barbara Chase-Riboud
  • Hank Willis Thomas with MASS Design Group
  • Wodiczko + Bonder & Maryann Thompson Architects
  • Yinka Shonibare
