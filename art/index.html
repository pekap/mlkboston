Please read our Request for Qualification call to artists to submit their portfolios and interest in designing our memorial. We will give artists until February 28, 2018 to send in their applications. To learn more, signup for our webinar (February 5tn, 7pm ET).

The Art Committee is co-chaired by Barry Gaither (National Center for Afro-American Art, and the MFA) and Karin Goodfellow (Director of the Boston Art Commission). Other Art Committee members are:

  • Brandon Terry, MLK Scholar, Professor, Harvard University
  • Cher Krause Knight, Public Art Scholar, Professor of Art History, Emerson College
  • Danielle Legros Georges, Boston Poet Laureate, Professor, Lesley University
  • Ekua Holmes, Boston Art Commission, and the Massachusetts College of Art and Design
  • L'Merchie Frazier, Director of Education, Museum of African American History
