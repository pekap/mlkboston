The country is divided, but Boston is united. With all that is going on in our nation today, we need the wisdom of Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. more than ever. It is time again to read his speeches and writings about racial equality, immigrant rights, and many other social issues.

The MLK Boston Committee is working with the City of Boston to create a world-class Martin Luther King, Jr. Memorial in Boston. This project was kicked off on September 20, 2017. (See Boston Globe story.)

The memorial will be designed to inspire visitors with MLK's words, and will also mention his time in Boston, as well as Boston's own complex history with race and civil rights.
