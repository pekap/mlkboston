in Boston

Paul English is working with the City of Boston and others to create a world-class Martin Luther King, Jr. Memorial in Boston. This project was kicked off on September 20, 2017. (See Boston Globe story.)

The memorial will be designed to inspire visitors with MLK's words, still relevant today with all that is going on in our nation and the world. The memorial will also mention his time in Boston, as well as Boston's own complex history with race and civil rights.
